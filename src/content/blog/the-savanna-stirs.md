Every good night with friends has a moment where someone leans in and says the
thing you didn't see coming. **Hoof & Claw** is built around that moment — and
then it does it to you, over and over, until the sun comes up.

It's a board game paired with a companion app that runs the table for you: it
deals the secret roles, keeps the phases moving, and resolves the night while you
do the only thing that matters — read the faces around you and decide who to
trust.

## What this dev log is

This is where we'll share the journey to Kickstarter: art drops, design notes,
playtest stories, and the occasional confession about a mechanic we had to throw
out. No rules spoilers, no homework — just the making-of.

If you want the updates in your inbox (and an early seat when we launch), the
waitlist is the place to be.

## What's next

- Finishing the illustrated faction reveals.
- A first look at the companion app in motion.
- Opening the waitlist for early-backer perks.

Welcome to the savanna. Trust no one. 🦓
